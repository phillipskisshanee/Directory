Searching for the hidden food so you don't have to

Are you tired of the same old food options presented at your favorite resturant? Do you want to look cool ordering something that no one else knows about? Is food basically your middle name? If you answered yes to any of these questions, then you have come to the right place. Secret Servings is a directory dedicated to revealing resturants in St. Louis with secret menus. We hope that you will be encouraged to travel throughout the city in search for these hidden treasures.
