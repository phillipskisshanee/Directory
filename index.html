BUSINESS OF THE WEEK

Serendipity Gallery is a art gallery that is located next to the Pageant. The gallery contains all handmade items and it prides itself in having majority local artists in the shop. Shoppers can find a wide variety of art available including: pottery, glasswork, jewelry, paintings, photographs and much more. Stop by Serendipity today to purchase some beautiful, handmade artwork!
